                            MYSORE TRIP


I wanted to visit close by places in Bangalore so I and my brother planned to visit Mysore by KSTDC 

(Karnataka State Tourism Development Corporation) -- "Mysore Sight Seeing"! package . 

 

The bus provided was very good and the driver was driving very safely.

 

The guide was provided for tour, he was very friendly and  informing us about next place we were to visit.

 

Bus stops at kstdc official restaurants

    - Karnataka Restaurant Maddur  ( at breakfast time )
    - Karnataka Hotels Mysore  ( at lunch time )

Food quality was good at both restaurants but food was not included in package, you have to buy food yourself 

 

They collect money for all tickets and other chrges beforehand in bus it self so you dont have to stand in queue and wait

 

We found this trip very nice. The service provided by KSTDC was excellent.
